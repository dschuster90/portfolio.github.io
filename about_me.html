A Little About Dave

I developed a passion for technology early on, largely influenced by my uncle's love for computer networking. He often had home projects, and I eagerly assisted him. Later, I stumbled upon a video on YouTube featuring Harvard's CS50 class. Intrigued, I delved into it and found myself captivated by the world of programming. And now, here I am, deeply engrossed in the field.
